Frequently Asked Questions

Is my therapy data used to train AI? +

No. Therapy session content is never used to train our AI models. We only use anonymized, aggregated data for improving matching algorithms, with explicit user consent.

Can I use the service completely anonymously? +

Yes. Our confessional platform requires no personal information. For therapy services, we require minimal contact information but offer encrypted communication options.

How do you prevent AI bias in therapist matching? +

We regularly audit our algorithms for bias, use diverse training data, and ensure human professionals review all matches. Users can also override AI recommendations.

What happens in a data breach? +

We notify affected users within 72 hours, provide free credit monitoring, and conduct a full security review. All data is encrypted, making it useless if accessed.

Can children use Psychezone services? +

Users under 18 require parental consent. We have specialized youth therapists and additional privacy protections for minors, including no AI matching for under-16s.
